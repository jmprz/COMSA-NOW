CSS Extension: Gawad Kalinga

The CCS extension program is a yearly event that focuses on a gift-giving initiative for the Gawad Kalinga community. It highlights the importance of generosity and engagement within the community while strengthening the connection between them and the students. The program also emphasizes the value of collaboration to uplift those in need making a deeper sense of purpose and social accountability among the participants.

Read More

COMSA YEP: Tech Crews, First Quest

The COMSA Year End Party celebrates the remarkable achievements of Computer Science Student Association leaders and imposes a deeper sense of leadership among them. It highlights the dedication of all student leaders under the program, recognizing their invaluable contributions to their respective roles. The event also focuses on strengthening the bonds within the community fostering a well connected and collaborative environment to each other.

Read More

CS Expo 2024: Unveiling New Possibilities and Creating New Realities

The event presents an exceptional opportunity for students to present their thesis projects, which employ diverse programming languages and techniques to enhance and create technological solutions for various industries. This event is primarily overseen by the institute’s fourth-year Computer Science students, with the overarching goal of inspiring a shift from routine to innovation, promoting automation, and contributing to global sustainability through the fields of science, innovation, and engineering.

Read More

Donation Drive: Typhoon Kristine

The organization Officers and students initiated a donation drive for the affected people of typhoon Kristine. The donated goods and clothes are delivered to the ABS CBN Foundation. The donated are goods such as sardines, noodles, and used/unused clothes.
