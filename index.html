About

Who we are

The name "Computer Science Association" has been a thriving force for all Computer Science students at EARIST for several years. It has successfully lived up to its name by leading the community in areas of leadership, teamwork, and maintaining a high standard of education. However, we aspire to go beyond these core responsibilities. In this ever-evolving technological landscape, we are dedicated to adapting and enhancing our commitment to the community.

Through a variety of events, workshops, and collaborative projects, COMSA provides opportunities for its members to dive deep into the latest innovations, whether it's artificial intelligence, blockchain, cybersecurity, or other groundbreaking technologies. It encourages creativity and the sharing of personal works, allowing members to showcase their projects, gain feedback, and potentially collaborate on future ventures. By emphasizing both personal and collective growth, the association nurtures a sense of community and promotes a culture of continuous learning, where everyoneâ€™s voice is valued, and the love for technology is shared by all. Ultimately, COMSA aims to empower its members to not only stay up-to-date with technological trends but also to play an active role in shaping the future of technology.

Objectives

Our community aims to create a fun and engaging space for tech enthusiasts to connect, collaborate, and expand their knowledge. We focus on internet functionality and emerging programming platforms, helping both beginners and experienced individuals stay ahead of trends. Through hands-on experiences, discussions, and projects, members develop practical skills while enjoying the learning process. Our goal is to make technology accessible, exciting, and foster lasting connections among those passionate about the digital future.

Mission

It aims to lead all Computer Science Students in leadership, teamwork, getting well known in information technology, enhancing computer literacy, and maintaining quality education by its faculty as well as in sports, arts, and spiritual activities.

Vision

To unite the computer science enthusiast at the institute, discuss the knowledge pertaining computer science technology, to bridge the gap between theoretical computer science and the real-world IT field, to intentionally act as the focal point for computer usage issues, foster a supportive environment for our members, and contribute positively to the broader community.
